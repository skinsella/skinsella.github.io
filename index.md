I am Full Professor of Economics at the University of Limerick, Ireland, Head of the Department of Economics, and Co-Director of [UL's Immersive Software Engineering](https://software-engineering.ie) programme. 

I am Economic Advisor to An Tánaiste and the Minister for Finance Simon Harris.

My research is archived at [ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7943-4797) and citations, etc., at [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Duqr7rIAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao). My academic CV is [here](https://rpubs.com/stephenkinsella/skcv) as HTML, and [here](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/5insg9zm5ohnyrcvcupex/SKCV.pdf?rlkey=wjx1dwrfasneipltfhm5aq8if&dl=0) as PDF. You can sometimes, although rarely, find me on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/stephenkinsella.bsky.social). I write a weekly blog at my [Substack](https://stephenkinsella.substack.com). 

I love writing, and AI won't stop me doing it. I was Chief Economics Writer for [The Currency.news](http://www.thecurrency.news) for five years, and before that a columnist with the *Sunday Business Post* for four years, and before that a weekly columnist with the *Irish Independent* for six years. Fifteen years of column writing, about 48 columns a year, has given me a specific expertise on the Irish economy.
