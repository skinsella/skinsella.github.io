**Introduction**

Macroeconomics is concerned with the analysis of aggregate fluctuations. The consequences for ordinary people when macroeconomic stability is compromised can be severe.

In this lecture, we'll study the case of Argentina in the 1990's. You can see from [Gapminder](http://www.gapminder.org) that Argentina has had a rough ride in terms of GDP per capita over the last 50 years. We will see examples of this in the story of
