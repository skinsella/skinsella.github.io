As posted on[Irish Economy](http://www.irisheconomy.ie/index.php/2011/01/22/denis-conniffe-rip/#comments) and on the [Geary Blog](http://gearybehaviourcenter.blogspot.com/2011/01/denis-conniffe-rip.html), Denis Conniffe has died. Sincere sympathies to Denis' family and friends. Denis was a top notch economist and statistician. [Take a look at his contributions here](http://scholar.google.com/scholar?hl=en&as_sdt=0,5&q=%22Denis+Conniffe%22). Denis was extremely kind to me when I returned from the US, and I won't forget that. I always enjoyed talking with him at IEA meetings, and for years we tried to get him down to UL to give a seminar on his innovative work.

All the best, Denis.
