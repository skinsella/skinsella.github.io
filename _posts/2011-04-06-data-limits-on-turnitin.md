title: "Data Limits on Turnitin"
date: 2011-04-06 09:24:31
categories:
  - UL
---

Some EC4024 students are experiencing issues when uploading files of greater than 20 megabytes to turnitin. This limit can't be changed, so ensure your file size is lower by saving the graphics you produce in low-resolution formats like .png. This should solve the issue.
