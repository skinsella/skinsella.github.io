The [CIFREM Doctoral programme at the University of Trento](http://cifrem.economia.unitn.it/doctoral/index.php) in Northern Italy is accepting applications for funded PhD positions for 3-4 years. If your research interests focus on Behavioural economics, Behavioural studies in management and organisations, Environmental economics, or International Economics, you should consider applying.

UL students can [email me](mailto:stephen.kinsella@ul.ie) for more information.

[Details about the course can be found here.](http://cifrem.economia.unitn.it/file/Microsoft%20Word%20-%20handbook.nov06.doc.PDF)
