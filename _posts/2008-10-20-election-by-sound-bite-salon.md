> [Joan Didion](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joan_Didion) cuts to the point of the US election: it is election by sound bite, election by media, and election by [Saturday Night Live](http://www.nbc.com/Saturday_Night_Live/). Anyone who saw [Palin on the show](http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/americas/us_elections_2008/7665644.stm)should by now know McCain will most likely be the next US president, trailing or not, simply because history repeats itself. I'd love to be wrong on this, but I don't think so this time. Read Didion's article to find out why.
