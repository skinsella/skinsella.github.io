In lectures today a student asked me by text message which economics book they should read to get acquainted with economics. I mentioned that this is, in my opinion, the best economics book ever written, by[Robert Heilbroner](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Heilbroner). Buy it, read it, and get smarter.
