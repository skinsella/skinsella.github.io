[Ian Wright](http://iwright.googlepages.com/home) is a researcher who combines non standard economic thinking with [statistical mechanics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistical_mechanics) and [Mathematica](http://www.wolfram.com/products/mathematica/index.html) simulations to draw out his points analytically. He's my kind of chap. When I grow up, I'd like to be a bit like him.[Check out his work](http://semanticsofmoney.blogspot.com/) to see why.
