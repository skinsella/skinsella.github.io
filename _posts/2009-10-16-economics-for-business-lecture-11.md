[I](http://www.irisheconomy.ie/agendaDEW2009.pdf)n this lecture we went over the concepts of [game theory](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Game_theory), [Nash equilibrium](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nash_equilibrium), Subgame Perfect Equilibrium, solution of games by underlining and [backward induction](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backward_induction), normal vs extensive forms, pure and [mixed strategies](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strategy_%28game_theory%29), and talked about the [microeconomic](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microeconomics) part of the module in summary. [You can look at the notes from the lecture here,](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/ec4004_2009_lecture11.key.pdf) you can watch the lecture here, and of course below.
