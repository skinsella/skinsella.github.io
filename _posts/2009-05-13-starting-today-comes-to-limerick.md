The fantastic Joan Mulvihil is bringing [Starting Today](http://startingtoday.ie/) to Limerick on the 21st of May in the Strand Hotel---h[ere are the details](http://startingtoday.ie/), and I highly recommend going along.

> Starting Today is a seminar for managers/professionals at all levels and from all disciplines impacted by recent redundancies and general career challenges in 2009. The purpose of the event is to provide a networking opportunity for great people to come together and find answers to career questions.
> Maybe it's time to consider further education, maybe its time to meet the person who can help you set up on your own, maybe you need CV and interviewing tips, or maybe it will simply be an inspiring evening that will give you the support and encouragement needed to get started.
> The point is that we get together and start something today!
