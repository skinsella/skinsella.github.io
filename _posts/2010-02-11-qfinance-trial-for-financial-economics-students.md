Students of EC4024, Financial Economics, and anyone else interested for that matter, can have a look at the free [QFinance](http://www.qfinance.com/home) widget on the [Financial Economics course page](http://www.stephenkinsella.net/teaching/ec4024_2010/). The widget at the bottom of the page allows you to search their whole website and financial dictionary, as well as gaining access to data and models. Well worth a look. Please leave a comment if you think it's useful, or if you think otherwise. Thanks to QFinance for setting this up for us.
