I've just finished correcting and checking the EC4024 midterms. They all look pretty good. Some general comments:

1. When students were asked to plug in numbers into a formula, there was typically no issue. This is why so few students failed.

2. When students were asked to interpret a formula or result, problems arose. To correct this, we'll be spending more time in tutorials on interpreting what the figures/formulas/etc. mean.
