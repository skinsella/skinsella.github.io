The President of the European Commission, [Jose Manuel Barroso](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/José_Manuel_Barroso), will visit the campus this coming Saturday, 19 September 2009 to give a lecture to students and staff. Details are going to be confirmed shortly. Economics of EU Integration and Economics for Business students should keep an eye on this site for more details. If you're around on Saturday, come and see him speak.

**Update.** Attendees are asked to be seated in the Millstream Common Room at 13h45.  An address by the President will be followed by a Question & Answer session.  The event will finish at 15h00 when refreshments will be served. Admission will be by ticket only as places are limited.  Those wishing to attend are asked to register (in person) for tickets at the UCH Box Office, Foundation Building.  Presentation of a student/staff ID card will be required for registration. Tickets will be available from tomorrow (Wednesday) morning at 10h00 to Friday noon.
