Messing about with [iShowU](http://shinywhitebox.com/home/home.html) over breakfast (which is why you hear my son asking for yogurt in the background), [I came across this visualisation engine for a website as web graph](http://www.aharef.info/static/htmlgraph/). This is a very cool tool, but what's even nicer is the way you can watch it evolve. StephenKinsella.net has over 1000 posts over 3 years with loads of uploaded [content](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Web_content) in the form of handouts and such, so it's a pretty big [graph](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graph_theory).

Also, any thoughts on the site? Should I leave it as is, or rdo it again? [You have your say.](mailto:stephen.kinsella@ul.ie)
