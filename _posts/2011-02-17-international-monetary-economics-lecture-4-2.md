This lecture looks at EMU, inflation, unemployment, and debt dynamics. We'll discuss Ireland's relative performance in terms of debt, and examine the macroeconomic issues. We'll also take a look at some numerical examples of the growth models I showed in lecture 3. The important reading for this lecture is by [Lane (2010)](http://bit.ly/e6hGyg).
