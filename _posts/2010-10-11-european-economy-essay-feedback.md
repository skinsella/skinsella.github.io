I'm anxious that European Economy students get as much practice in, and [feedback](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/feedback1.pdf) from, writing their essays. That's why you'll each get a feedback sheet, with students scoring below a C1 offered the chance to revise and resubmit their work for increase marks. In tutorials next week, you'll go through your essays with Mary, and we'll produce anonymised examples of 'excellent' and 'poor' essays for you to learn from, as well as excellent and poor reference lists, etc. You are all formally volunteered for this sampling, anyone that doesn't wish to have their essays used in the pool of sample questions we use, drop me [an email](mailto:stephen.kinsella@ul.ie) by 5pm today.
