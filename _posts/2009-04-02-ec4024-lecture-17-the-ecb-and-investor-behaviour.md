Tomorrow's lecture will review the functions of the ECB and discuss the transmission mechanisms of monetary policy with regard to the interaction of the central bank and very large, institutional investors. We'll use pensions as an example of a very large institutional fund which has [direct effects on the population](http://www.thepost.ie/story/?jp=mhsnaukfid) when things go wrong.
