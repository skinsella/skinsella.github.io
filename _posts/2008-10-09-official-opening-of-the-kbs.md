Many of you will have been down to the new [Kemmy Business School](http://www.ul.ie/business/), or KBS, which was officially opened this week with a brilliant lecture by Prof. *Gearóid* Ó Tuathaigh The new building houses the new [experimental economics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Experimental_economics) laboratory, of which much, much more later, as well as a state of the art trading simulation floor and [marketing technology](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marketing_research)suites, and a new HRM lab. The offices are pretty swish, too.
