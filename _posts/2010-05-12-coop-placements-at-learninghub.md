Jennifer Moroney Ward is the Director of the[Northside Learning Hub](http://learninghub.ie) in Limerick. There are placements available on some of their programmes, which I think would suit a business student on COOP, in particular. These programmes are entrepreneurial in spirit, and, in addition to being part of the LearningHub's learning buddies programmes and digital media programmes, I understand Jennifer is really interested in developing a business plan for their new music space within a framework of sustainability/non profit model. [Here](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/MUSIC-HUB-DISCUSSION-DOCUMENT.docx) are the kinds of things the music space might be involved in (.docx). If you're interested, drop [Jennifer an email](Jennifer@learninghub.ie).
