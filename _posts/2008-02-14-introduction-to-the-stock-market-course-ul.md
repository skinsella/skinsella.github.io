EC4024 students might be interested in this course, which provides trading simulation training for 150 bucks. Details below the fold.

FEXCO Stockbrokers Course - Introduction to the Stock

Market

This course is aimed at people who have an interest in the stock

market. It is designed to give confidence in the understanding of the function

and benefits of the stock exchange. It has a practical focus and features how to

use a simulation account, which allows you to practice trading while not risking

any money. 

Beginners and experienced investors have found

benefits in the course in the past. On completion of the course, participants

will have an opportunity of forming an investment club to put their newly

acquired knowledge to practical use.

Start Date: Tuesday 4th March 2008

Time: 7.30pm-9.30pm

Duration: 8 weeks

Fee: €150

Venue: University of Limerick
