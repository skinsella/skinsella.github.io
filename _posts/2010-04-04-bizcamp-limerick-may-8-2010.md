[BizCamp](http://bizcamp.ie) is coming back to Limerick!

The venue will be the [Kemmy Business School, University of Limerick](http://maps.google.com/maps?client=safari&rls=en&q=Kemmy%20Business%20School%2C%20University%20of%20Limerick&oe=UTF-8&um=1&ie=UTF-8&sa=N&hl=en&tab=wl). All are welcome, admission is free.

Don’t know what a BizCamp is? [Find out here](http://www.bizcamp.ie/about/).

BizCamp will run from 10am to 4pm on Saturday, May 8, 2010 in the Kemmy Business School.

[Signup for the event now.](http://www.bizcamp.ie/register-limerick/)If you’re not sure if BizCamp is for you, [read this](http://www.bizcamp.ie/who-is-bizcamp-for/). Here is some [general info on giving BizCamp talks](http://www.bizcamp.ie/speakers-and-talks/).

[Register to attend here](http://www.bizcamp.ie/register-limerick/)! We’re looking forward to your talk!
