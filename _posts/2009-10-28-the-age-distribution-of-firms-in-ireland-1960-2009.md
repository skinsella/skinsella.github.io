Abstract. This paper studies the age distribution of firms in Ireland from 1961 to 2009 using data on over 159,000 firms. I build a model which mixes a [lognormal distribution](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Log-normal_distribution) of firm size with an [exponential distribution](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Exponential_distribution) of firm age to explain the [evolution](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolution) of these data. The model is fitted using [maximum likelihood estimation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maximum_likelihood), and tested using [Kolmogorov-Smirnov](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kolmogorov%E2%80%93Smirnov_test) [statistics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistics). The model replicates observed [empirical](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Empirical) regularities.
