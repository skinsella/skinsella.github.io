**Introduction**

This is an interim lecture about stagﬂation, a situation we might actually ﬁnd ourselves in sooner rather

than later. We’ll deﬁne stagﬂation, look at episodes of stagﬂation in recent economic history, and study a

model to explain and perhaps manage stagﬂation in the macroeconomy. We’ll relate the insights of these

models to policies you might see implemented in the US Economy in the near future, and talk about what this might mean for Ireland's economy going forward. Get your slides and handouts while they're hot.
