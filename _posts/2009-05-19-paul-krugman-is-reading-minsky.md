Expect the econo-blogosphere to go nuts tearing Minsky apart or trying to put him together, [now that PK is on the case](http://krugman.blogs.nytimes.com/2009/05/19/actually-existing-minsky/). 

I've given some lectures on Minsky's ideas [as applied to the Irish case](http://www.stephenkinsella.net/?s=minsky), but the best place to get the man's ideas is in his[magnum opus](http://www.amazon.com/Stabilizing-Unstable-Economy-Hyman-Minsky/dp/0071592997/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1242731218&sr=8-1). Which I think is pretty well written, myself.
