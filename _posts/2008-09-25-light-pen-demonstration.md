Here's a little video showing something [Finbarr Murphy](http://www.ul.ie/business/people/index2.php?action=pview&id=118)put together for his classes here at [UL](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Underwriters_Laboratories). It's based on the light pen ideas of[Johnny Chung Lee](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~johnny/) from [CMU](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/College_Music_Update), and you can see the thing working below. The basic idea is to use an led light source, tracked by a wiimote, to translate movements of the led pen into images on a computer, displayed on the projector you see. So it's like you're writing on the wall. For about 40 euros.

[Light Pen Doo Hickey](http://vimeo.com/1806071?pg=embed&sec=1806071) from [Stephen Kinsella](http://vimeo.com/kinsells?pg=embed&sec=1806071) on [Vimeo](http://vimeo.com?pg=embed&sec=1806071).
