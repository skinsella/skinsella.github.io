This lecture looks at central banks, what they are, what they are supposed to do, and their role in the stabilisation (or de-stabilisation) of business cycles. The slides are below, as are some interesting readings. We'll be going through the maths written [in this paper](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/cycles.pdf), which eventually became a chapter in Kinsella & Leddin, 2010.
