Here's a talk I gave last night in Nenagh's art's centre. It's the first talk given in the centre since it's refurbishment, and naturally a lot of the talk was about Ireland's impending series of loans from the IMF. About 60 people showed up, I was expecting 20. The discussion after the talk was excellent, and I came away having learned a lot, and feeling energised, rather than depressed about the current situation.
