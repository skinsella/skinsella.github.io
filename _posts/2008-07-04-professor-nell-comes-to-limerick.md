Professor Edward Nell of the [New School for Social Research](http://www.newschool.edu/nssr/) is coming in the spring [as a Fulbright professo](http://www.fulbright.ie/cms/publish/annual_awards/US_Awardees_2008.php)r. I'm chuffed, and I think he will make a positive contribution to the research and teaching objectives of the department.

More details on Prof. Nell's work and his books [are here](http://cepa.newschool.edu/het/profiles/nell.htm), and his New York Review columns [are here](http://www.nybooks.com/authors/3671). His books in print [are here](http://www.amazon.com/s?ie=UTF8&search-type=ss&index=books&field-author=Edward%20J.%20Nell&page=1).

Incidentally, UL has 5 of the 29 awards in this category. Nice!
