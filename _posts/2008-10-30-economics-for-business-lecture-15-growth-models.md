The [Solow Growth model](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Exogenous_growth_model) attempts to explain the features of growth we encountered in the last lecture. We need to be able to explain movements in [capital accumulation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_accumulation), labour force growth, and technology.
