[EC4004](http://www.stephenkinsella.net/teaching/ec4004-economics-for-business-autumn-2008/)students will remember Dr [Aidan Kane](http://www.aidankane.net)'s guest lecture a few weeks ago, [slides here](http://www.stephenkinsella.net/2008/11/24/aidan-kanes-guest-lecture-online/), when he suggested that Ireland was fiscally *dans la merde*, and this situation would get worse once the November tax returns are in. 

Guess what? They're in. [Here the figures are,](http://www.finance.gov.ie/viewdoc.asp?DocID=5559) and they are not pretty reading. We are 7,894,761 euros in the red today, and it's getting worse.
