Here's a chapter from my forthcoming[book](http://www.amazon.com/Designing-Strategy-Irish-Economic-Recovery/dp/184218198X/ref=sr_1_5?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1266838034&sr=1-5) with Anthony Leddin. All comments are most welcome.

> Abstract. This chapter examines the interaction of Ireland’s national debt and price levels using a simple model. The chapter discusses the role of uncertainty in making Ire- land’s financial system robust to severe downturns caused by asset deflations, and makes constructive suggestions to limit future credit bubbles.
