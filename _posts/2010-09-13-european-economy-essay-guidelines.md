Below are some essay guidelines for the Essays you'll do in EC4027, European Economy.

Instructions. You must produce a 5 page essay on a topic agreed between yourself and the lecturer, and submit the hardcopy to the box marked “EC4027 Essays”, near the Department Office, KB 3-22a by the assigned deadline.

You must also submit a copy of your essay to turnitin using the file naming convention EC4027_Essay_Yourname_yourstudentnumber.doc. No lateness will be tolerated. You will lose 10% of your mark per day of lateness.

I do not want regurgitated wikipedia articles. I want your thoughts. Do not plagiarize, I will check for plagiarism using Turnitin.com. You’ll receive written feedback on your book review. I urge you to read widely, write notes, talk about the subject of your essay with your friends, think about it before writing, and write an essay of not less than 4, and not more than 5, A4, typed pages, with 1.5 line spacing, 1 inch margins, in font Times New Roman, with your name, your student number, and your email. Staple the pages together in the top left hand corner. There is no need for report booklets or binding.

You'll get lots of tips and techniques for writing in the tutorials you'll attend this semester.
