[Irish Social Science Data Archive](http://www.ucd.ie/issda/) 

The establishment, within the auspices of the [Geary Institute](http://www.ucd.ie/geary) (formerly the    Institute for the Study of Social Change), of the Irish Social Science Data Archive (**ISSDA**),    fills a long recognised need in Ireland. **ISSDA** holds an    ever-increasing quantity of machine-readable data from surveys and official    statistics (such as the Census) and makes them readily available    to users in the academic, public and commercial sectors.

This will be very useful for the EC6012 class next semester.
