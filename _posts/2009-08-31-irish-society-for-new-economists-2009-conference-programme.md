UL staff, and especially UL students thinking about doing research work in the social sciences, are invited to ISNE 2009:

> The Irish Society of New Economists (ISNE) will host its sixth annual conference at the Kemmy
> Business School, University of Limerick, on Friday 2nd October 2009. The conference is intended to
> provide a forum for new economists starting Ph.D. research or professional careers in Ireland.
> We are pleased to announce that there was a record number of applications this year; and we are
> looking forward to three parallel sessions on the day of the conference. The provisional programme
> (as at 31st August) is available[here](http://docs.google.com/fileview?id=0B4jLC5fXYQdCODVlZmRiNTYtMzlhNy00OWJiLTk5NDgtNWFlYTFkYWRmOTBl&hl=en).
