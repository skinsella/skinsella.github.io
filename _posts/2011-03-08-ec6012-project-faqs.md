Apparently there is confusion around the inclusion of appendices, etc, 
into the page count. 

 I'd like you to *include* the appendices you generate as part of the 
page count, meaning the piece should not be 10 pages of text and 30 
pages of graphs, etc. This is an exercise in compression. That said, 
I'm not going to fail anybody that produces 10.5 pages, either. Just 
don't over do it. The FAQs for EC4024 also apply to the EC6012 
assignment. 

 Please note: do *not* use a cover page or any identifying information 
in your turnitin.com submission, you'll be docked marks. 

 Please *do* use a coverpage when submitting your project in hardcopy 
to the box on the 3rd floor of the KBS. 

 Any other questions, please feel free to contact Mary or myself.
