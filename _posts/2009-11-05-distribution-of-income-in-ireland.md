This week in 2 classes (EC4004 and EC4333), I talked about[Lorenz curve](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lorenz_curve)s, [Gini](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gini_coefficient) distributions, and [income inequality](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economic_inequality).

The [recent OECD report](http://www.oecd.org/document/35/0,3343,en_2649_33733_43948003_1_1_1_1,00.html) shows the inequality in who's contributing to [income tax](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Income_tax) receipts.

The chart shows [Ireland](http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=53.3441666667,-6.2675&spn=10.0,10.0&q=53.3441666667,-6.2675%20%28Republic%20of%20Ireland%29&t=h) is a very unequal country when it comes to paying tax-- the rich pay most.
