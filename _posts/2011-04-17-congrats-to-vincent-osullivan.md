My PhD student Vincent O'Sullivan has a good reason to celebrate this weekend. 

 Every year Emerald invites each journal’s Editorial Team to nominate 
what they believe has been that title’s Outstanding Paper and up to 
three Highly Commended Papers from the previous 12 months. 

 Vincent's paper has been included among these. His article entitled 
“What caused the Irish banking crisis” published in Journal of 
Financial Regulation and Compliance has been chosen as an Outstanding 
Paper Award Winner at the Literati Network Awards for Excellence 2011. 

 Well done Vincent, I'm chuffed for you.
