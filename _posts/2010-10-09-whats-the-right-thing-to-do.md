This week in Economics for Business we spent quite some time thinking about whether one course of action was preferable to another, specifically in the [case of the fire brigade watching a chap's house burn down](http://www.stephenkinsella.net/2010/10/04/let-it-burn/). Here's a nice lecture by Harvard Prof. [Michael Sandel](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Sandel) (ht [Geary chappies](http://gearybehaviourcenter.blogspot.com/2010/10/libtertarianism-sandel-harvard-justice.html)) on this exact topic: what's the right thing to do? If I remember correctly, I took 2 straw polls this week, the first class was 80:20 for allowing the guy's house burn down, the second class was 66:33 for allowing it to burn.
