In 1962 and 1963, three papers were written incorporating international trade into the IS-LM model. The development of the model is recounted by Nobel Laureate Robert Mundell [here](http://www.columbia.edu/~ram15/). This model was the workhorse for Keynesian open economy macro for an academic generation, and we'll go over it in the lecture. The lecture is a stripped down version of [this talk](http://www.ssc.wisc.edu/~cengel/).

[Here's a handout of the slides](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2009/03/ec4006-mundell-fleming-handout.pdf) and the slides themselves [are on slideshare](http://www.slideshare.net/kinsells).
