Two thoughts on today's bond disaster: first, this isn't the work of one finance professor writing an opinion piece in a newspaper and [watching it catch fire on a blog](http://www.irisheconomy.ie/index.php/2010/09/02/lucey-on-anglo-loss-sharing/). Really. Second, the spin about ebbs and flows just doesn't make sense: no one else experienced funding cliffs [like this](http://bit.ly/9rgxNX) today. We saw spreads on Anglo move out 36% today. We are looking at evidence of a systematic problem.
