While searching my computer for a review of Wynne Godley's Monetary Economics by Lance Taylor titled 'The Foxy Hedgehog', I came across this article I wrote waaay back in 2001 as an undergrad for the [Student Economic Review](http://www.tcd.ie/Economics/SER/index.php). Yes, I was a nerd back then, too. The editor of the Student Economic Review that year was one [Ronan Lyons](http://ronanlyons.com), who has, sadly, faded into complete obscurity.

[Anyway, here's the little essay on econometric methodology.](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Kinsella_Hedgehog_logic.pdf)It goes a bit mad on the quotes, but you'll get the idea. The article won best original essay that year. Ten years ago (jaysus).
