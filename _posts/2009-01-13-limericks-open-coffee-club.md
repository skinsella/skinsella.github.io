Readers interested in creating business opportunities in Limerick should consult [Open Coffee Club Limerick](http://opencoffeeclublimerick.com/?page_id=2) for networking opportunities in the Shannon region. From the blog:

Open Coffee Club meetings are informal “gatherings” of entrepreneurs, start-ups, investors and anybody with an interest in business and technology to tell their story, discuss topics, exchange points of view and generally have a good, relaxing time.
