If you are doing a PhD, thinking of doing one, or you've completed and want to discuss your work in a constructive and collaborative environment, then please consider registering for ISNE 2009, to be held here in UL at our new business school. 

> The Irish Society of New Economists (ISNE) will host its sixth annual conference at the University of Limerick on Friday 2nd October 2009. The conference is intended to provide a forum for new economists (in post-graduate research programs or the private and public sectors) to present papers from any area of economics. This forum is intended for anybody in the early stages of research - eligibility to present has nothing to do with age. We strongly encourage those working on economics-related research in all settings to present.
