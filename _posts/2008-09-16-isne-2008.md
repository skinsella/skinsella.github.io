[![National University of Ireland, Galway](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/c/ce/NUI%2C_Galway.png)](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image:NUI%2C_Galway.png)

The Irish Society for New Economists, ISNE, is having its annual conference in NUI, Galway on October the 3rd. Martin Ryan of [UCD](http://www.ucd.ie) and John Cullinan of [NUIG](http://www.nuigalway.ie/) are organising it.

[Full details are here.](http://isne08.googlepages.com/)

I'm chairing a session, and [Dominic Trepel](http://healtheconomic.blogspot.com) is presenting the first part of his thesis. 

It will be an interesting session, and I'm looking forward to it.
