Tomorrow [Fintan O'Toole](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fintan_O'Toole), Irish Times Deputy Editor, [Author](http://www.amazon.co.uk/Ship-Fools-Stupidity-Corruption-Celtic/dp/0571252680), [Angry chap,](http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/features/2009/1106/1224258186386.html) and all-around [ledge-bag](http://www.irishtimes.com/search/index.html?rm=listresults&filter=datedesc&keywords=Fintan+O%27Toole&x=0&y=0&irelandcom_searchdefault=Search+Hotels%2C+Plumbers%2C+Restaurants%2C+etc.&irishtimescom_searchdefault=) will be giving a seminar from 4.30 to 6 in GL1-01 in the Library. Everyone interested in Irish public policy and public life should head along, it will be well worth it.

Update: [A recording of the seminar is here](http://tomfelle.com/Site/Journalism_Podcasts/Entries/2010/2/3_Fintan_OToole.html).
