[The Irish Economy.ie](http://www.irisheconomy.ie/) has the potential to be a real intellectual resource, because its content is being given by people like [Philip Lane](http://www.philiplane.org/), [Kevin O'Rourke](http://www.tcd.ie/Economics/staff/orourkek/homepage.htm), [Karl Whelan](http://www.karlwhelan.com/), and[Alan Ahearne](http://www.nuigalway.ie/staff/alan_ahearne/index.html). 

Unlike the Geary Blog, which has a research focus most of the time, this blog seems more geared to op-ed type pieces, which elicit discussion by forum members and blog readers, mcuh like Martin Wolf's FT blog.

The writing is current affairs-focused, and informed, but accessible, so I've high hopes for this blog. Let's all read with interest.
