On Friday we'll be looking at [Trade Creation and Trade Diversion](http://internationalecon.com/Trade/Tch110/T110-2A.php), concepts that define whether a trade agreement is, in a very specific sense, 'good' or 'bad'. They were developed by Jacob Viner, a professor at U. Chicago and one of the foremost trade theorists of his generation.

Click [here](http://www.econlib.org/library/Enc/bios/Viner.html)to browse his online works. For those interested, look in particular at [Studies in the Theory of International Trade. 1937](http://www.econlib.org/library/NPDBooks/Viner/vnSTT.html), and[Free Trade and Customs Unions](http://www.econlib.org/library/Enc/FreeTradeAgreementsandCustomsUnions.html).
