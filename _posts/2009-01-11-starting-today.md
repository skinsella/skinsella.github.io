This is an interesting conference bringing recently unemployed people together with entrepreneurial experts, [exactly what I recommended the government do for Dell en masse a few days ago](http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/opinion/2009/0109/1231406001604.html).

Readers should spread the word about it, perhaps we could think about running it in Limerick as well at a later date.

From the site:

> Starting Today is a half-day seminar for managers/professionals impacted by recent down-sizing and redundancies and impending career challenges in 2009. The purpose of the event is provide a networking opportunity for great people (accountants, solicitors, IT professionals, HR managers, general managers, marketing professionals, administrators etc
