This is a new initiative I'm involved in, as well as [newbeginning](http://www.newbeginning.ie/).

The idea is pretty simple.  *The People’s Economy* aims to provide all citizens with the tools to understand Ireland’s economic situation by

- turning econo babble into everyday language
- providing the answers to frequently asked questions regarding our economy
- identifying and busting common myths about what is happening in Irish economics
- suggesting key questions to ask candidates

[Find out more here.](http://thepeopleseconomy.com/)
