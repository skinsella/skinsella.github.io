This lecture will introduce financial economics students to the concepts of Irish corporate governance, and [corporate social responsibility](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corporate_social_responsibility). The goal is to show how lax corporate governance and financial fragility as defined by [Minsky](http://cepa.newschool.edu/het/profiles/minsky.htm) interact. Given the recent episodes in our indigenous banking sector, this lecture is pertinent, timely, and practical.
