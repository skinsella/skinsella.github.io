The Irish Undergraduate Awards have just launched and I would encourage our students to both register for the awards and to submit their coursework from this year and previous years. The awards provide a wonderful opportunity for our students to be recognised on a national stage for the excellent work that they do each year.

Each year over 100 undergraduates from across Ireland will be honoured at an annual awards ceremony. There are many other opportunities available to students who produce interesting and engaging work throughout the year. For full details please go to [www.iuawards.ie](http://www.iuawards.ie/).
