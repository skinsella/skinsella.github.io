This lecture introduces and critiques the [Efficient Markets Hypothesis](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Efficient-market_hypothesis), the adaptive markets hypothesis, and describes the implications for our re-regulated financial system as a result of the recent series of crashes. Slides are [Here](http://stephenkinsella.net/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/EC4024_2010_Lecture9.pdf). Some cool readings are below.[Here is a recording of the lecture](http://www.vimeo.com/9676537).

[Burton Malkiel](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burton_Malkiel), [The Efficient Market Hypothesis and its Critics](http://www.princeton.edu/~ceps/workingpapers/91ma)

Neely et al[, The Adaptive Markets Hypothesis: Evidence from the Foreign Exchange Market](http://research.stlouisfed.org/wp/2006/2006-046.pdf)

[Andrew Lo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrew_Lo),[The Adaptive Markets Hypothesis: Market Efficiency from an Evolutionary Perspective](http://web.mit.edu/alo/www/Papers/JPM2004_Pub.pdf)
