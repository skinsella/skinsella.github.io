UL economics students should make a point of attending this lecture by 
one of Ireland's most distinguished journalists. 

 "A Life Less Ordinary: Editing The Irish Times" 

 An open interview and questions and answers session with Geraldine 
Kennedy will take place at 2pm in the Irish World Academy Theatre (large 
theatre 1) on Wednesday, March 23 at 2pm sharp. 

 The event is open to all.
