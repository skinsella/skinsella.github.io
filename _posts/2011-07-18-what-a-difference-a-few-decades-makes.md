Here's a cool chart I made using[this software](http://www.media.mit.edu/research/groups/macro-connections). It compares Ireland in 2009 and Ireland in 1962. Click on the image to enlarge it. You see the sectors that are 'big' in the economy in 1962, and those that are 'big' in 2009. The multinational presence is obvious, as is the continued presence of agriculture and services.
