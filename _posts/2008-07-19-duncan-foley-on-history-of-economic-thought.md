[Duncan Foley](http://cepa.newschool.edu/~foleyd/), who taught me advanced [microeconomics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microeconomics) and mathematics, and in my opinion the best economic educator around, gives a lecture on the [history of economic thought](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4XhvG_fD0HA) in his development.

[You can watch the lecture here](http://msl.stream.yorku.ca/mediasite/Viewer/Viewers/ViewerVideoOnly.aspx?mode=Default&peid=f82febf5-9459-4fcd-8d83-fe3ac2b5c992&pid=57b754ae-700b-48ef-96ac-c54a13a2be66&playerType=SL1#), but you may need to download a silverlight [plugin](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plugin). It's well worth your time.

Foley's latest book,[Adam's Fallacy, is here](http://www.amazon.com/Adams-Fallacy-Guide-Economic-Theology/dp/0674027299/ref=pd_bbs_sr_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1216501977&sr=8-1).
