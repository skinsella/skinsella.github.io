[Here are 3 lectures by Professor Joan Robinson from 197](http://www.agsm.edu.au/bobm/iows/jrobinson.html)4. [Joan Robinson](http://www.econlib.org/library/Enc/bios/Robinson.html) was a member of JM Keynes' inner circle as he wrote [The General Theory](http://www.amazon.com/General-Theory-Employment-Interest-Money/dp/3941579282%3FSubscriptionId%3D0PZ7TM66EXQCXFVTMTR2%26tag%3Dstephkinse-20%26linkCode%3Dxm2%26camp%3D2025%26creative%3D165953%26creativeASIN%3D3941579282), and later became a strident critic of the textbook economics students still learn today. [Her work](http://books.google.com/books?as_auth=Joan+Robinson&source=an&ei=AnjfSbi9NI-1-Qasiuj_CA&sa=X&oi=book_group&resnum=4&ct=title&cad=author-navigational) was deep and wide ranging, and it is, frankly, a pleasure to hear her voice after years of reading her papers.
