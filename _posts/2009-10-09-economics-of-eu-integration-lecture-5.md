Assuming an EMU, what are the [economic](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economy) effects of one? We saw last week that the [EU](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union) was almost certainly not and [OCA](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optimum_currency_area), yet the EU exists as a functioning [currency](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Currency) area. What are the likely effects of this on growth amongst [member states](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Member_State_of_the_European_Union)? We developed the canonical model used to describe [economic growth](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economic_growth), and expose this model to the facts. We also looked at capital and factor market integration.
