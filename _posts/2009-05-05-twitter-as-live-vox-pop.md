[George Lee](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Lee_%28journalist%29) has decided to run for a seat in the [Dail](http://www.oireachtas.ie/) (the best discussion of the impact of an economically literate TD so far has been at the [Irish Economy](http://www.irisheconomy.ie/index.php/2009/05/05/george-lee-enters-politics/#comments)), but one aspect I'd like to focus on is the reaction on [Twitter](http://twitter.com) to George's decision. Want a live [vox pop](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vox_populi) in less than 140 characters? [Look no further than a twitter search, like this one](https://twitter.com/#search?q=george%20lee). Of course, you can look at many other things (swine flu, for example), but as George is hawt right now, take a look at what the twittersphere has to say. It might surprise you.
